About Project

Our project, is dealing with real-life problem that is being faced by Youtubers and companies/brands these days, right now many of the brands wants to hire YOUTUBER for their product promotion, brand promotion, charity, live shows, influencing and much more. So, the brands who are facing problem that how to reach to the youtuber that they want to hire, we are trying to help them. Here, as a team we gathered all the problems that they are facing, And, built an application named “TUBERS” (a marketplace for YOUTUBERS) which YOUTUBERS can use for self-promotion by contacting team TUBERS and brands & charity can use for hiring their favorite YOUTUBER. Users/Brands just have to register themselves on the application and have to visit to their Youtuber profile and have to fill the form that is there on the profile of each Youtuber and when they submit the form, then that specific youtuber or team will further contact them
